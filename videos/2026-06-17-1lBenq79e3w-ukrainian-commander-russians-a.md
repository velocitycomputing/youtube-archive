## Summary

**What Was Discussed:**
Ukrainian military commanders warn that Kostyantynivka, a key city in Donetsk, faces imminent capture by Russian forces within weeks to months. Russian forces are advancing through infiltration tactics—sending small groups of 1-2 soldiers to gradually overwhelm defenses—while Ukrainian units struggle to hold the line. Between 100-250 Russian soldiers have already infiltrated the city. Russia's strategy involved moving forces west around the Klyven Bik reservoir, breaching Ukrainian defenses through weaker positions (particularly the newly-formed 156th Mechanized Brigade), and spreading through the western portion of the city. Russian media acknowledges continued Ukrainian resistance in the northeastern sector, but the overall trend favors Russian advances. If Kostyantynivka falls, Russia will be positioned closer to the strategic hubs of Kramatorsk and Sloviansk.
