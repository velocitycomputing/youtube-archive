Watch for an imminent Ukrainian counteroffensive while Russian forces are weakened, likely occurring before Russia's planned fall mobilization when fresh troops arrive. The broader actionable insight is that Ukraine has found a path to territorial victory without massive casualties—by systematically destroying Russian supply infrastructure, they can force withdrawals and establish defensible positions. Monitor Ukrainian announcements closely over the next month, as commanders appear poised to announce additional major operations.
