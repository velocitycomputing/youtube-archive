## Summary

Google reported record earnings ($109.9B revenue, 22% YoY growth; Google Cloud hit $20B with 63% growth), driven entirely by AI across their ecosystem. The White House is proposing pre-release vetting of all AI models after OpenAI's GPT-5.5 demonstrated breakthrough capabilities in cybersecurity that leapfrogged government abilities—a moment the hosts call the "Mythos moment." Most critically, compute is now severely constrained: even Google admits they lack spare compute for competing projects and must internally auction capacity between search, cloud, and DeepMind weekly. Companies like Orin now sell "compute futures" as demand fundamentally outpaces supply.

Consider chip and energy stocks, which are now the economy's constraint.
