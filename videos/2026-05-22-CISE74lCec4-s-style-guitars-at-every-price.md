## Summary

Jonathan Lindsey at Tap Street Music showcased three S-style guitars across different price tiers. S-style guitars are solid-body electric guitars featuring a double cutaway, bolt-on maple neck, three pickups (typically single coils and humbuckers), and a tremolo bridge. The featured guitars were: the Yamaha Pacifica 112 (entry-level budget option with two single coils and a humbucker, praised for beginners), the PRS SE Silver Sky (mid-range John Mayer signature model with three single coils and a vintage-inspired aesthetic), and the Novo Idris (premium Nashville-built boutique guitar with tempered pine body, roasted maple neck, Lindy Fralin pickups, and full customization options). Each was played and demonstrated to highlight tonal and quality differences across price points.

If you're shopping for an S-style guitar, match your budget to one of these three tiers: start with the Pacifica 112 if you're a beginner with limited budget and want versatile pickup options; choose the PRS SE Silver Sky if you want noticeably better quality and vintage single-coil tones without spending premium prices; invest in the Novo Idris if budget allows and you want a fully customizable, hand-built instrument with boutique components. Beyond these three, also explore alternatives from Jet, Ibanez, Eastman, and Duesenberg, which offer their own takes on the S-style design.
