The speaker discusses a leaked Iran nuclear deal obtained by Bloomberg, characterizing it as worse than previously understood. JP Morgan anticipates a potential spike as soon as July or August. Position expectations accordingly, as supply-demand fundamentals (extremely low inventories combined with high global demand) will eventually force prices upward when market conditions stabilize.
