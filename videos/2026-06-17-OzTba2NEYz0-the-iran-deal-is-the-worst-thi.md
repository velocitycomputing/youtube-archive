## Summary

The speaker analyzes the Iran deal's memorandum of understanding (MOU) section by section, arguing it is catastrophically one-sided diplomacy. The speaker emphasizes that this represents extracting maximum concessions from the US with zero meaningful Iranian concessions—a defining failure in diplomatic negotiation.
