## Summary

Trump appeared on NBC's Meet the Press where he made numerous false claims: that the Iran blockade isn't a war, that stationed troops face no danger (contrary to 13 deaths and billions in costs), that keeping troops there costs "very little," that gas prices will plummet immediately, and that farmers struggling with fertilizer costs should be satisfied with the $28 billion he gave them years ago for China tariffs. If you support fair elections, push your state to count ballots quickly (Minnesota and Wisconsin do this while maintaining access) so bad-faith actors can't exploit delays to spread misinformation.
