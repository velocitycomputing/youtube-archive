## Summary

Use the two-week subscription window immediately to architect and build the core of any complex project you care about, leveraging Fable 5's superior capabilities for the hardest, most intelligence-intensive work. Write modular, well-documented code designed to be maintained by cheaper models (Opus, open-source, local models) afterward—break your codebase into manageable blocks so future work doesn't require Fable 5's expensive API. For $200, you'll accomplish an insane amount of work, learn how to use advanced models strategically, and build a foundation that's economical to maintain long-term; after two weeks, revert to cheaper tools for incremental updates and maintenance work.
