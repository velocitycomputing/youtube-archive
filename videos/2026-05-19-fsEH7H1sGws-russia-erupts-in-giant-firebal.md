## Summary

Russia launched a devastating aerial attack on Kyiv on May 14, 2026, deploying 56 missiles and 675 drones that killed 24 civilians (including children) and injured 48 others. Ukraine's air defenses intercepted most of the barrage but some breached through, striking residential buildings across the country. The contrast demonstrates that Ukraine maintains moral and strategic superiority by focusing on infrastructure while Russia wastes resources on civilian terror. For sustained impact, monitor Ukraine's expanding drone partnerships and manufacturing capacity, as these capabilities will continue escalating pressure on Russian logistics and energy supplies.
