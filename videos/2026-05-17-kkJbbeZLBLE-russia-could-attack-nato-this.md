## Summary

Study Ukraine's anti-corruption reforms and ecosystem development (the Brave One marketplace and domestic component production) as the blueprint for "dronification" of NATO forces, not just drone procurement. NATO leadership should establish formal knowledge-sharing with Ukraine's defense ministry to learn concentrated drone tactics and counter-drone strategy. Take the potential Russian summer offensive seriously as a strategic warning while closing the capability gap that currently leaves the West unprepared for modern drone-intensive warfare.
